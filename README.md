# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard using Power BI

The dataset is sourced from **Kaggle** and includes detailed information about movies and TV shows available on Amazon Prime Video.

### 🔢 Key Highlights:

* 🎥 **Total Titles:** 9,655
* ⭐ **Aggregate Ratings:** 25 categories
* 🎭 **Genres Covered:** 519
* 🎬 **Directors:** 5,771
* 📅 **Time Span:** 1920 – 2021

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies

* 📊 **Power BI** – for data visualization and dashboard creation

---

## 🚀 Key Features of Dashboard

* 📈 Year-wise growth of content
* 🎭 Genre distribution analysis
* ⭐ Ratings breakdown
* 🎬 Director-wise contribution
* 📺 Movies vs TV Shows comparison

---

## 🧠 Insights You Can Gain

* Rapid growth of content in recent years
* Popular genres dominating the platform
* Distribution of ratings across titles
* Contribution of different directors over time
