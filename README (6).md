## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes the growth and content trends of Amazon Prime Video from **1920 to 2021**.

It helps users understand:

* How the platform has evolved over time
* Distribution of movies vs TV shows
* Trends in ratings, genres, and directors

The dashboard is designed to provide **quick insights through visual storytelling**, making complex data easy to understand.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset is sourced from **Kaggle** and includes detailed information about movies and TV shows available on Amazon Prime Video.

### 🔢 Key Highlights:

* 🎥 **Total Titles:** 9,655
* ⭐ **Aggregate Ratings:** 25 categories
* 🎭 **Genres Covered:** 519
* 🎬 **Directors:** 5,771
* 📅 **Time Span:** 1920 – 2021
